📋 What They Actually Do

Tecnoglass manufactures glass, windows, and architectural glass systems primarily from its 5.8 million square foot facility in Barranquilla, Colombia. The company exports almost entirely to the U.S. market (95% of revenues), with 85% concentrated in Florida's construction markets.

Background/History: Founded by the Daes brothers and taken public via SPAC in 2013. Despite claims of geographic diversification, the company remains heavily dependent on Florida's construction cycle, which is now showing signs of deterioration.
